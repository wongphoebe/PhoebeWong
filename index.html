My Favourtie City

My favourite city is Hong Kong, which is also my hometown and is often affectionately referred to as "Home Kong".
Renowned by being the "Pearl of the Orient", it is a vibrant metropolitan located at the South-East coast of China.

If you ever have a chance to visit Hong Kong, I would recommend the following items on your must-do list:

  • FOOD: From fine-dining to street snacks, you will find cuisine from different countries, not to mention the most authentic local Hong Kong style.
  • SHOPPING: Treat yourself with a tax-free shopping experience.
  • HIKING: Countryside of Hong Kong are designed with well-facilitated hiking routes and are generally easy to access by public transport.
